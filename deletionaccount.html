⚠️ Account Deletion

Boneloss — AI-Powered Dental Bone Loss Detection

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

1. Account Deletion Request

We respect your right to delete your account and all associated data from the Boneloss application. This page explains the process and what data will be affected.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All your data will be permanently removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

2. How to Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion using any of the following methods:

  1. From the App: Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Delete Account and follow the on-screen prompts to confirm deletion.
  2. Via Email: Send an account deletion request to support@simats.edu from the email address registered with your account. Include the subject line: "Account Deletion Request — Boneloss App".

3. Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and role.
  • Uploaded Radiographs: All dental X-ray images you uploaded for analysis.
  • Analysis Reports: All AI-generated analysis results, bone loss measurements, severity classifications, and treatment recommendations.
  • Patient History: All saved patient records and case studies.
  • Saved Cases: Any saved or bookmarked study cases.
  • App Preferences: All settings, notification preferences, and security configurations.

4. Data Retention After Deletion

ℹ️ Your data will be deleted within 30 days of your request. During this period, your account will be deactivated and you will not be able to access the App.

Certain anonymized, aggregated data that cannot be traced back to you may be retained for research and analytical purposes in compliance with applicable laws.

5. What Happens After Deletion

  • You will be immediately logged out of all devices.
  • Your account credentials will no longer work.
  • You will not receive any further notifications or emails from the App.
  • If you wish to use the App again in the future, you will need to create a new account.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about account deletion or need assistance, please contact us:

  • Email: support@simats.edu
  • Institution: SIMATS University, Chennai, India
